Sumit gupta Sgp
Speak your Heart out! I have seen my students going through myriad emotions due to different types of people in their lives. Sometimes it is easy to say directly what is going inside our head and heart, but life puts us through a gamut of gory situations where we fail to speak what we think and realize. To put it point-blank, basically we become afraid thinking about undesirable consequences our statements could force us into. But is it always ok to let our soul and conscience face situations that worsen our mental conditions, increase agony and make us feel helpless and miserable? As a human being I would say 'NO' because by staying quiet, we are hurting our self-respect and somehow acting as a catalyst for those persons who love to inflict pain, trouble and hardships. Hence, I urge and request everyone reading this post to share their experiences and bring to the fore the tussle and torment they had to go through in their lives. Note: All the passout students are free to take the name of the person with negative qualities and attributes but the students currently studying are advised not to mention the names of any person explicitly in their own interest. Though they could drop some hints like any peculiar characteristic, funny names given to them or department name etc so that we can relate with them more consummately. One last thing, any criticism, suggestions or comments pertaining to my way of teaching, my behaviour or me in any aspect is most welcome and greatly anticipated. Dear all, Speak Dil se...Your SGP sir is all ears. Don't just like this post but please share your experiences over here in comments so that few people could understand what they have done and are still doing.
